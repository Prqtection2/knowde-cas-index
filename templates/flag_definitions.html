About TSCA Flags

TSCA (Toxic Substances Control Act) flags indicate specific regulatory status, exemptions, or restrictions for chemical substances. These flags help users understand the regulatory context of each chemical in the database.

Flag Definitions

5E
TSCA Section 5(e) Order

Indicates a substance that is the subject of a TSCA section 5(e) order.

5F
TSCA Section 5(f) Rule

Indicates a substance that is the subject of a TSCA section 5(f) rule.

12C
Export Prohibition

Indicates a substance that is prohibited to be exported from the United States under TSCA section 12(c).

FRI
Free-Radical Initiator

Indicates a polymeric substance containing no free-radical initiator in its Inventory name but is considered to cover the designated polymer made with any free-radical initiator regardless of the amount used.

PE1
1995 Polymer Exemption (1,000-10,000 daltons)

Indicates a polymer that has a number-average molecular weight of greater than or equal to 1,000 daltons and less than 10,000 daltons and that is exempt under the 1995 polymer exemption rule. The polymer's oligomer content must be less than 10 percent by weight below 500 daltons and less than 25 percent by weight below 1,000 daltons.

Indicates a polymer that has a number-average molecular weight of greater than or equal to 10,000 daltons and that is exempt under the 1995 polymer exemption rule. The polymer's oligomer content must be less than 2 percent by weight below 500 daltons and less than 5 percent by weight below 1,000 daltons.

PE3
1995 Polymer Exemption (Polyester)

Indicates a polymer that is a polyester and that is exempt under the 1995 polymer exemption rule. The polyester is made only from monomers and reactants included in a specified list that comprises one of the eligibility criteria for the 1995 polymer exemption rule.

PMN
Premanufacture Notification

Indicates a commenced PMN substance.

R
Risk Management Rule

Indicates a substance that is the subject of a proposed or final TSCA section 6 risk management rule.

S
Significant New Use Rule (Final)

Indicates a substance that is identified in a final Significant New Use Rule.

SP
Significant New Use Rule (Proposed)

Indicates a substance that is identified in a proposed Significant New Use Rule.

T
Test Rule (Final)

Indicates a substance that is the subject of a final TSCA section 4 test rule or order.

TP
Test Rule (Proposed)

Indicates a substance that is the subject of a proposed TSCA section 4 test rule or order.

XU
Chemical Data Reporting Exemption

Indicates a substance exempt from reporting under the Chemical Data Reporting Rule, (40 CFR 711).

Y1
1984 Polymer Exemption (>1,000 daltons)

Indicates a polymer that has a number-average molecular weight greater than 1,000 and that was exempt under the 1984 polymer exemption rule.

Y2
1984 Polymer Exemption (Polyester)

Indicates a polymer that is a polyester and that was exempt under the 1984 polymer exemption rule. The polyester is made only from reactants included in a specified list of low-concern reactants that comprises one of the eligibility criteria for the 1984 polymer exemption rule.
